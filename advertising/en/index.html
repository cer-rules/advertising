[Advertising Rules]

  • 1. Server rules must be strictly observed
  • 2. Advertising for the same server must be limited to once a month
  • 3. Advertising by the same person must be limited to once a week
  • 4. Sending media files via links is prohibited
  • 5. Troll-related advertising is prohibited

[Punishment for Violations]

  • Violation of Articles 1, 2, 3, and 4
    • ・Timeout 1 hour
    • ・Warning +1
  • Violation of Article 5
    • ・Permanent ban
